You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals above. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your UserScore to the random Computer Guess. You lose the game if your total score goes above the Computer Guess.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it, and the value will change after a win or loss.
